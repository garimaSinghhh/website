About Us

Why to choose Us?

Firstly, the company has over four decades of experience in the automotive industry, which means that they have a wealth of knowledge and expertise to draw from. This experience is reflected in the high-quality services they offer, which are second to none.
Secondly, Highland Tourist Industrial Garage LTD has a team of highly skilled and experienced technicians who are committed to providing exceptional services to their clients. Their team of technicians is well-equipped with the latest tools and technology to ensure that they provide efficient and high-quality services that meet the specific needs of their clients.
Thirdly, the company's focus on customer satisfaction is evident in the quality of their work. They work closely with their clients to understand their specific needs and offer customised solutions that meet those needs. This attention to detail ensures that clients are happy with the work done on their vehicles and are likely to return for future services.
Fourthly, Highland Tourist Industrial Garage LTD offers a broad range of services that cater to various needs in the automotive industry. From customisation and conversion of tourist vehicles to modification of new vehicles, all types of insurance accident repairs, auto body and trim repairs, automotive mechanical and electrical repairs, panel beating and spray painting, metal body building, and upholstery, the company has a solution for every need.
Finally, the company's location makes it convenient for clients to access their services. Their garage is situated in a central location that is easily accessible, making it easy for clients to drop off and pick up their vehicles.
In summary, choosing Highland Tourist Industrial Garage LTD is a wise decision because of their extensive experience, highly skilled and experienced technicians, focus on customer satisfaction, broad range of services, and convenient location.
