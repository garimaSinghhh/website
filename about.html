About Us

Who is Highland Tourist Industrial Garage Ltd?

Highland Tourist Industrial Garage Ltd is a leading indigenous company with more than forty years of experience in the automotive industry. We are well-known for providing exceptional services in the customisation and conversion of tourist vehicles, modification of new vehicles, all types of insurance accident repairs, auto body and trim repairs, automotive mechanical and electrical repairs, panel beating and spray painting, metal body building, and upholstery.

Our specialty in customisation and conversion for tourist vehicles is unmatched. Our team of highly skilled and experienced technicians takes pride in creating customised solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients. We work closely with our clients to design and convert vehicles that cater to their specific requirements. Our attention to detail and dedication to customer satisfaction is evident in the quality of our work.

At Highland Tourist Industrial Garage LTD, we also offer modification services for new vehicles. Our expert technicians use the latest technology and equipment to modify vehicles to meet the latest industry standards. Our focus on innovation and excellence is evident in the quality of the modifications we make to vehicles.

We understand that accidents happen, and that's why we offer all types of insurance accident repairs. Our team of expert technicians uses cutting-edge equipment and technology to provide efficient and high-quality repairs, restoring vehicles to their pre-accident condition.

We are also equipped to handle all types of auto body and trim repairs. Our technicians use advanced technology and techniques to repair and restore damaged bodywork and trim to its original condition, leaving no trace of the damage.

Our expertise in automotive mechanical and electrical repairs is top-notch. Our team of experienced mechanics uses the latest diagnostic tools and equipment to identify and repair mechanical and electrical faults, ensuring that vehicles operate optimally.

Highland Tourist Industrial Garage LTD also offers panel beating and spray painting services, ensuring that vehicles are restored to their original aesthetic condition. Our skilled technicians use high-quality paints and materials, ensuring that vehicles look as good as new.

Finally, our metal body building and upholstery services ensure that our clients' vehicles are comfortable and durable. Our team of expert craftsmen uses high-quality materials to create customised metal bodies and upholstery, tailored to the specific needs of our clients.
