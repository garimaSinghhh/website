Highland Tourist Industrial Garage Ltd

Visit Us

Highland Tourist Industrial Garage Ltd is a leading indigenous company based in Nairobi, Kenya with more than forty years of experience in the automotive industry. We are well-known throughout the country for providing exceptional services in the customisation and conversion of tourist vehicles, modification of new vehicles, all types of insurance accident repairs, auto body and trim repairs, automotive mechanical and electrical repairs, panel beating and spray painting, metal body building, and upholstery.

Services We Offer

HTIG offers a comprehensive range of services to keep your vehicles in top condition. Our tourism services provide efficient and high-quality workmanship with a focus on attention to detail and customer satisfaction. Our commercial services prioritize fast, efficient, and high-quality on-site repairs for a variety of vehicles. Skilled mechanics provide a wide range of general services, from routine maintenance and repairs to upholstery and seat recovery. Trust HTIG to keep your vehicles running smoothly and looking their best.
