Our Services

Services We Offer

Here are the services provided by our company:

TOURISM

HTIG has over 25 years of experience in the tourism industry, making them experts in their field. The company strives to provide a balance of quality and quantity in their services, ensuring full customer satisfaction.

The company is dedicated to using the latest technology and techniques to provide efficient and high-quality workmanship in all their services. With a focus on attention to detail and customer satisfaction, HTIG has become a reputable and reliable choice for those seeking services in the tourism industry.

COMMERCIAL

We understand the importance of keeping your vehicle in top condition, which is why we prioritize fast, efficient, and high-quality on-site repairs. Our comprehensive maintenance portfolio covers a wide range of vehicles, including vans, SUVs, and salon vehicles. This portfolio includes a variety of repair services, such as van repairs, tail lift repairs, general bodywork, paint jobs, engine maintenance, and accident damage repair. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service and ensuring that all repairs are carried out to the highest possible standards. Trust us to keep your vehicle running smoothly and looking its best.

GENERAL

Our skilled mechanics have the expertise to handle all types of requirements and modifications for your vehicle, including routine servicing, MOTs, brakes, steering, electronic diagnosis, engine/gearbox rebuilds, and combelt replacements. We go beyond just mechanical services, offering additional services such as upholstery, seat recovery, and seat repairs for all types of vehicles, from buses and siren cars to tour vehicles. With our wide range of services, you can trust us to keep your vehicle running smoothly and looking its best.
